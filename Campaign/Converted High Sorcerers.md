**HP** 90 each (12d8 + 36)

**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned  
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12  
**Languages** understands Elvish and Common but can’t speak

### **Traits**
**Undead Sorcery.** Cha is the spellcasting ability (save DC 15, +7 to hit). No verbal components.  
**Arcane Feedback.** When reduced below half HP, the sorcerer’s spell damage increases by +2 per die.

### **Spellcasting** (Simple Loadout)
**At Will:** *fire bolt* (necrotic), *ray of frost*  
**3/Day Each:** *blight, fear, misty step*  
**1/Day Each:** *blight* or *fireball* (necrotic)

### **Actions**
**Necrotic Bolt.** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +7 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d10) necrotic damage.

### Bonus action
Can teleport a converted elf

### **Tactics (Optional)**
Run them as a unit: two cast *blight*, one uses *fear*, one repositions with *misty step*. No concentration overlap needed.
