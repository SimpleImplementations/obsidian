# **Bone Seer of the Withered Glade**

## **Bone Seer of the Withered Glade**
*Elven archmage converted by Orren el Putrefacto*  
*Medium undead, lawful evil*

**AC** 17 (natural armor, arcane ward)  
**HP** 110 (13d8 + 52)  
**Speed** 35 ft.

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|10 (+0)|16 (+3)|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|16 (+3)|14 (+2)|

**Saving Throws** Con +7, Int +7, Wis +6  
**Skills** Arcana +7, Perception +6, Stealth +6  
**Damage Vulnerabilities** bludgeoning  
**Damage Resistances** piercing, necrotic  
**Damage Immunities** poison  
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned  
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 16  
**Languages** understands Elvish and Common but can’t speak  
**Challenge** 8 (3,900 XP) **PB** +3

### **Traits**
**Undead Spellweaver.** Intelligence is the spellcasting ability (save DC 15, +7 to hit). No verbal components.  
**Withered Majesty.** Creatures of its choice starting their turn within 10 ft. must succeed on a DC 15 Wis save or have disadvantage on their first attack roll that turn.  
**Grave-Bound Focus.** Advantage on Constitution saves to maintain concentration.  
**Elven Fade.** In dim light or darkness, the first attack against it each round is made at disadvantage.

### **Spellcasting**
9th-level spellcaster.  
**At Will:** *chill touch, minor illusion, mage hand, detect magic*  
**3/Day Each:** *blight, counterspell, fear, misty step*  
**2/Day Each:** *blight; blinding smite* (necrotic, no radiant); *summon undead* (ghostly or skeletal only)  
**1/Day Each:** *blight* ×3  
> **Blight Obsession.** *Blight* may deal necrotic damage instead; undead allies in the area heal HP equal to half the damage dealt (rounded down).

### **Actions**
**Grave Bolt.** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +7 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (4d8) necrotic damage; target can’t regain hit points until the start of the Bone Seer’s next turn.  
**Sovereign of Stillness (Recharge 5–6).** Each chosen creature within 20 ft. must succeed on a DC 15 Wis save or be restrained until the end of its next turn; on a success, its speed is halved until the end of its next turn.

### **Bonus Action**
**Veil of the Fading Court.** Heavily obscured until the start of its next turn, or until it attacks or casts a spell.

### **Reaction**
**Arcane Deflection.** When hit or failing a save, gain +3 to AC or the save, potentially turning failure into success.

### **Description**
Once a radiant elven loremistress, this being was converted—not slain. Orren el Putrefacto stripped away voice and mercy, but not memory. The Bone Seer judges intruders with ancient, inevitable magic. When Bone Rangers hunt, the Bone Seer decides who must die.
