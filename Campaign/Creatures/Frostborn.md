Frostborn Goblins inhale crystalline vapors from ancient magical glaciers. This reshapes their minds into cold, lucid networks of intuition. They communicate without words, act without hesitation, and summon frost-spirits shaped like hungry wolves. Their minds are quiet as snowfall. They do not fear death, because they have already seen it.

### Traits

***Iceborn Resilience.*** When a creature within 5 feet of the goblin hits it with a melee attack, that creature takes 3 (1d6) cold damage.

***Glacial Sight.*** The goblin perceives flickers of possible futures shaped by arcane frost. It cannot be surprised while conscious and has advantage on initiative rolls. It also has resistance to psychic damage.

***Magic Resistance.*** The goblin has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

***Frost Agility.*** The goblin has advantage on Dexterity saving throws.

***Spellcasting.*** The goblin is a 5th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks). It requires no material components.

- **Cantrips (at will):** [*ray of frost*](https://www.aidedd.org/spell/ray-of-frost)
- **1st level (4 slots):** [*armor of Agathys*](https://www.aidedd.org/spell/armor-of-agathys), [*ice knife*](https://www.aidedd.org/spell/ice-knife)
- **2nd level (3 slots):** [*mirror image*](https://www.aidedd.org/spell/mirror-image)
- **3rd level (2 slots):** [*sleet storm*](https://www.aidedd.org/spell/sleet-storm), [*ice storm*](https://www.aidedd.org/spell/ice-storm)

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** The goblin makes two attacks: one spell attack or dagger attack, and one dagger attack.

***Dagger.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

***Ray of Frost.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +4 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (2d8) cold damage, and the target's speed is reduced by 10 ft. until the start of the goblin's next turn.

### Bonus Actions

***Conjure Frost Worg (Recharge 6).*** The goblin summons a [[Frost Worg]] into an unoccupied space within 30 ft. The Frost Worg acts immediately after the goblin's turn, obeys its commands, and lasts for 1 minute or until reduced to 0 hit points.
