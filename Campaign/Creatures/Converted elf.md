**Damage Resistances** piercing, necrotic

**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned

**Languages** understands Elvish and Common but can’t speak  
**Challenge** 2 (450 XP)

### **Traits**
**Undead Agility.** Can Dash, Disengage, or Hide as a bonus action each turn.  
**Forest Stalker.** Advantage on Dex (Stealth) checks in lightly obscured terrain (foliage, dim light, ruins).  
**Ambush Marksman.** Weapon attacks made while hidden deal +7 (2d6) damage.  
**Relentless Hunter.** When a creature damaged this turn moves more than 10 ft. before the start of the ranger’s next turn, the ranger can use its reaction to move up to half its speed toward it.

### **Actions**
**Multiattack.** Two attacks with bone shortsword or bone longbow.  
**Bone Shortsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) slashing.  
**Bone Longbow.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing.  
**Shadow Step (Recharge 5–6).** Move up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks, then make one weapon attack at advantage.

### **Description**
Once elven scouts and rangers, these undead retain a hunter’s memory—how to stalk, when to strike, and when to fade back into shadow. Converted by Orren el Putrefacto, they serve as tireless sentinels and executioners, haunting forests and ruins with unnatural patience.

## **Lair Actions (Bone Ranger Territory)**
On initiative count 20 (losing ties), one effect occurs; the same effect can’t be used two rounds in a row.

**Grasping Undergrowth.** A 15-ft. square within 60 ft. erupts with roots, vines, or skeletal remains. Creatures in the area must succeed on a DC 13 Str or Dex save (creature’s choice) or have speed reduced to 0 until the start of the next round.

**Hunter’s Shadows.** Dim light fills a 20-ft. radius centered on a point within 60 ft. until the next initiative count 20. Orren’s undead have advantage on Stealth checks in the area.

**Bone Whispers.** One creature within 60 ft. must succeed on a DC 13 Wis save or have disadvantage on its next attack roll or saving throw before the end of its next turn.
