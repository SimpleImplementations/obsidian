**Languages:** —  
**Challenge:** **2 (450 XP)**

---

### **Traits**

**Spider Climber.** The swarm can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing an ability check.

**Swarm.** The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any space large enough for a Small creature. The swarm cannot regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

**Silent Scurry.** The swarm has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to move quietly or hide.

**Pack Frenzy.** The swarm has advantage on attack rolls against any creature it is sharing a space with.

---

### **Actions**

**Swarming Claws.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +5 to hit, reach 0 ft., one target in the swarm’s space.  
_Hit:_ **15 (5d4 + 3) slashing damage**, or **8 (2d4 + 3)** if the swarm has half its hit points or fewer.

**Skittering Rush (Recharge 5–6).**  
The swarm surges in a frantic wave of motion. Each creature within 10 feet must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or take **10 (3d6)** slashing damage and be **knocked prone**. On a success, the creature takes half damage and isn’t knocked prone.

---

### **Description**

When a nest of bone creepers awakes, they move as a writhing, scraping mass of skeletal goblin remnants. Their tiny claws scratch stone and wood, filling passages with a dry chittering echo. Swarms typically infest ruined tunnels, crypt ceilings, or rafters — dropping as a nightmarish tide.
