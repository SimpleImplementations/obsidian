### Traits

***Sure-Footed.*** The yak has advantage on Strength and Dexterity saving throws made against effects that would knock it prone.

***Cold Adaptation.*** The yak is adapted to cold climates and has resistance to cold damage.

***Charge.*** If the yak moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a Gore attack on the same turn, the target must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the yak can make one Hooves attack against it as a bonus action.

***Mountain Strider.*** El yak ignora el terreno difícil causado por superficies rocosas, pendientes pronunciadas, nieve o grava suelta. Además, tiene ventaja en tiradas de salvación contra efectos que lo empujarían o derribarían mientras esté en terreno montañoso.

*Si se desea una versión más simple:* ***Paso Montañés.*** El yak no considera terreno difícil el terreno montañoso natural.

### Actions

***Gore.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage.

***Hooves.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one prone creature. *Hit:* 15 (2d10 + 4) bludgeoning damage.
