Bone Creepers are animated goblin skeletons twisted into predatory shapes. They cling to walls and ceilings, dropping onto prey with skittering, insectlike frenzy.

### Traits

***Spider Climber.*** Can climb difficult surfaces, including ceilings, without an ability check.

***Silent Bones.*** Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to move silently.

***Opportunistic Predator.*** If it starts its turn hidden from a creature, its first claw attack that turn deals +3 (1d6) slashing damage.

### Actions

***Claw.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing.

***Pounce (Recharge 5–6).*** Target within 10 ft. must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity save or take 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage and be knocked prone; on a success, half damage and no prone.
