*Goblin convertido en esqueleto por Orren el Putrefacto*  
*(opcional: Swarm of Bone Creepers)*  
*Small undead, chaotic evil*

**AC** 14 (natural armor)  
**HP** 8 (2d6 + 1)  
**Speed** 40 ft., climb 40 ft.

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|8 (−1)|16 (+3)|12 (+1)|6 (−2)|14 (+2)|5 (−3)|

**Skills** Perception +6, Stealth +7  
**Damage Vulnerabilities** bludgeoning  
**Damage Resistances** piercing  
**Damage Immunities** poison  
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned  
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 16  
**Languages** understands Goblin but can’t speak  
**Challenge** 1/4 (50 XP)

### **Traits**
**Spider Climber.** Can climb difficult surfaces, including ceilings, without an ability check.  
**Silent Bones.** Advantage on Dex (Stealth) checks to move silently.  
**Opportunistic Predator.** If it starts its turn hidden from a creature, its first claw attack that turn deals +3 (1d6) slashing damage.

### **Actions**
**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing.  
**Pounce (Recharge 5–6).** Target within 10 ft. must succeed on a DC 12 Dex save or take 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage and be knocked prone; on a success, half damage and no prone.

### **Description**
Bone Creepers are animated goblin skeletons twisted into predatory shapes. They cling to walls and ceilings, dropping onto prey with skittering, insectlike frenzy.
