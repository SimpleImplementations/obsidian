**Attract Beasts**  
_3rd-level enchantment (ritual)_  
**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self (300-foot radius)  
**Components:** V, S, M (a tuft of fur and a drop of honey)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

You exude a calming magical presence that draws the attention and trust of nearby beasts. Roll 9d10; the total is how many hit points of beasts this spell can affect. Creatures are affected in ascending order of their current hit points (ignoring unconscious creatures), chosen from among beasts within 300 feet of you.

Each affected beast is charmed and compelled to move toward you by the safest route. When a beast reaches a space within 10 feet of you, it becomes non-hostile toward you and your allies for 1 hour, unless it is harmed or witnesses one of you harming another beast.

If an affected beast takes damage before reaching you, the spell ends for that creature.

While non-hostile, the beast will not attack or flee from you, and may allow peaceful interaction such as being fed or examined, at the DM’s discretion.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, roll an additional 2d10 for each slot level above 3rd.

**Classes:** Druid, Ranger
