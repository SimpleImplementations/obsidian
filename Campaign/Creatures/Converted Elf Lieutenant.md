## **Lieutenant: Bone Ranger Captain**
*Elfo explorador veterano convertido en esqueleto por Orren el Putrefacto*  
*Medium undead, lawful evil*

**AC** 17 (natural armor)  
**HP** 85 (10d8 + 40)  
**Speed** 40 ft.

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|12 (+1)|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|11 (+0)|16 (+3)|9 (−1)|

**Saving Throws** Dex +7, Wis +6  
**Skills** Perception +7, Stealth +9, Survival +7  
**Damage Vulnerabilities** bludgeoning  
**Damage Resistances** piercing, necrotic  
**Damage Immunities** poison  
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned  
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 17  
**Languages** understands Elvish and Common but can’t speak  
**Challenge** 4 (1,100 XP)

### **Traits**
**Undead Commander.** Friendly undead within 30 ft. gain +2 damage on their first attack each turn.  
**Shadow Skirmisher.** Can Dash, Disengage, or Hide as a bonus action each turn.  
**Marked Prey.** At the start of its turn, choose one creature within 60 ft.; the first weapon hit that turn deals +7 (2d6) damage.

### **Actions**
**Multiattack.** Three attacks with bone longsword or bone longbow.  
**Bone Longsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 4) slashing.  
**Bone Longbow.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 4) piercing.  
**Crippling Shot (Recharge 5–6).** Target within 120 ft. must succeed on a DC 14 Con save or take 13 (3d8) piercing damage and have its speed reduced by 20 ft. until the end of its next turn.

### **Reaction**
**Relentless Pursuit.** When a creature it can see moves more than 10 ft., the captain moves up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

### **Description**
Bone Ranger Captains retain echoes of battlefield leadership, directing lesser undead with silent gestures and uncanny positioning. Under Orren’s command, they act as executioners rather than scouts—ending hunts they begin.
