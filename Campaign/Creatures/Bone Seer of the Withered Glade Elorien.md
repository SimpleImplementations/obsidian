### Traits

***Undead Spellweaver.*** Int is the spellcasting ability (save DC 18, +10 to hit). No verbal components.

***Withered Majesty (Aura).*** Enemies of the Bone Seer that start their turn within 15 ft. must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom save or have disadvantage on attack rolls until the start of their next turn.

***Grave-Bound Focus.*** Advantage on Constitution saves to maintain concentration.

***Elven Fade.*** In dim light or darkness, the first attack against the Bone Seer each round is made at disadvantage.

### Spellcasting

12th-level spellcaster.

**At Will:** *chill touch*, *mage hand*, *minor illusion*, *detect magic*

**3/Day Each:** *blight*, *counterspell*, *fear*

**2/Day Each:** *blight*; *blinding smite* (necrotic); *summon undead*

**1/Day Each:** *finger of death*

***Blight Obsession.*** *Blight* can deal necrotic damage; undead allies in the area heal HP equal to half the damage dealt.

### Actions

***Grave Bolt.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +10 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 22 (5d8) necrotic; target can't regain HP until the start of the Bone Seer's next turn.

***Sovereign of Stillness (Recharge 5–6).*** Creatures of choice within 30 ft. must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom save or be restrained until the end of their next turn; on a success, speed is halved.

### Legendary Actions

*Legendary Action Uses: 3. The Bone Seer can take one legendary action at a time, at the end of another creature's turn.*

***Cantrip.*** Cast *chill touch*.

***Fade Step.*** Teleport up to 20 ft. to dim light or darkness.

***Grave Pulse (Costs 2).*** Creatures of choice within 15 ft. take 10 (3d6) necrotic damage (DC 18 Constitution save for half).

### Lair Actions (Withered Glade)

On initiative count 20 (losing ties), one effect occurs:

***Rooted Silence.*** A 20-ft. radius becomes difficult terrain until the next round; enemies have disadvantage on concentration saves.

***Necrotic Bloom.*** A blighted tree erupts; enemies within 15 ft. take 14 (4d6) necrotic damage (DC 18 Constitution save for half).

***Judgment of the Dead.*** One restrained or frightened creature takes 18 (4d8) necrotic damage.
