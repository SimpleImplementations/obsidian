### Traits

***Frost Aura.*** A creature that starts its turn within 5 ft. of the Worg takes 2 (1d4) cold damage.

### Actions

***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (2d6 + 2) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) cold damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
