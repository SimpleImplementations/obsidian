


### 1. Eternal Dimness
While under the storm:
- All **bright light becomes dim light**
- All **dim light becomes darkness**
- Nonmagical light sources have **half normal radius**
- Creatures relying on sunlight-based traits gain **no benefit** from them
### 2. Necrotic Saturation (Healing Rule – Refined)
All magical healing is **flattened**:
- Healing restores **maximum die + ability modifier**
- Healing **cannot remove conditions** (poisoned, frightened, exhaustion, etc.)
Narrative flavor:
- Wounds close, but flesh looks grey and stiff
- Healing magic feels _cold and reluctant_
### 4. The Storm Drains Color and Sound

Perception and stealth subtly shift.

- **Wisdom (Perception) checks relying on sight** are made at **disadvantage**
    
- **Dexterity (Stealth) checks** made in dim light gain **advantage**
    

Narrative:

- Colors are muted
    
- Sound carries strangely
    
- You hear things before you see them
    

💡 _This changes how players approach scouting without math._
