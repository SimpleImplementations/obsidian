---

### **Traits**

**Forest Guardian.** Thalanor has advantage on attack rolls and saving throws while touching natural ground or vegetation.

**Animate Roots (Recharge 5–6).** Roots erupt in a 20-ft. radius centered on Thalanor. Each creature of his choice in the area must succeed on a DC 15 STR save or be restrained until the end of its next turn.

**Siege Monster.** Thalanor deals double damage to objects and structures.

**Ancient Vitality.** If reduced below half HP while in a forest, Thalanor regains 10 HP at the start of his next turn (once per combat).

---

### **Actions**

**Multiattack.** Thalanor makes two Slam attacks.

**Slam.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
Hit: 18 (3d8 + 5) bludgeoning.

**Hurl Boulder.** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, range 60/180 ft., one target.  
Hit: 24 (4d10 + 5) bludgeoning.

**Grasping Branches (Recharge 6).** Branches lash out at up to two creatures Thalanor can see within 15 ft. Each must succeed on a DC 15 STR save or be grappled (escape DC 15) and restrained until the grapple ends.

---

### **Reactions**

**Rooted Defiance.** When Thalanor takes damage, he can reduce the damage by 10 by hardening his bark.

---

### **Tactics**
Thalanor opens combat by **controlling terrain** with *Animate Roots*, then focuses on **isolating threats** using Grasping Branches.  
He prioritizes enemies wielding fire or harming the forest and fights until driven from his sacred grove.

---

### **Description**
Thalanor Rootwarden is an ancient ent bound to sylvan lands older than elven memory. He speaks slowly, weighs every action, and sees violence as a last resort—but once the forest is threatened, his wrath is as inevitable as the turning of seasons.
