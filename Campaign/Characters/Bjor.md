### **Traits**
**Beast-Focused Druid.** Bjor has 3 uses of Wild Shape per day. He almost always fights in beast form.  
**Barkskin Ritual.** If given 1 round before combat, Bjor casts *barkskin* (AC 16) before Wild Shaping.  
**Primal Resilience.** When a Wild Shape drops to 0 HP, Bjor reverts with remaining damage and can Wild Shape again as a bonus action.

### **Actions (Orc Form)**
**Quarterstaff.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning.  
**Healing Word (3/day).** Bonus action; a creature within 60 ft. regains 8 (1d4 + 4) HP.  
**Wild Shape.** Bonus action to transform into a beast (see below).

### **Tactics**
Bjor opens with **Barkskin → Wild Shape**, then commits to **one form at a time**, using only basic attacks.  
He retreats only if forced out of Wild Shape twice.

### **Description**
Bjor believes beasts are the purest expression of the land's will. Civilization is weakness; tooth, tusk, and claw are truth. When he fights, he abandons speech entirely and lets the wild speak for him.
