## **Bone Seer of the Withered Glade (Ascendant)**
*Elven archmage converted by Orren el Putrefacto*  
*Medium undead, lawful evil*

**AC** 18 (natural armor, arcane ward)  
**HP** 165 (22d8 + 66)  
**Speed** 35 ft.

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
|10 (+0)|16 (+3)|20 (+5)|20 (+5)|18 (+4)|14 (+2)|

**Saving Throws** Con +10, Int +10, Wis +9  
**Skills** Arcana +10, Perception +9, Stealth +8  
**Damage Vulnerabilities** bludgeoning  
**Damage Resistances** piercing, necrotic  
**Damage Immunities** poison  
**Condition Immunities** exhaustion, poisoned  
**Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 19  
**Languages** understands Elvish and Common but can’t speak  
**Challenge** 12 (8,400 XP) **PB** +4

### **Traits**
**Undead Spellweaver.** Int is the spellcasting ability (save DC 18, +10 to hit). No verbal components.  
**Withered Majesty (Aura).** Enemies of the Bone Seer that start their turn within 15 ft. must succeed on a DC 18 Wis save or have disadvantage on attack rolls until the start of their next turn.  
**Grave-Bound Focus.** Advantage on Con saves to maintain concentration.  
**Elven Fade.** In dim light or darkness, the first attack against the Bone Seer each round is made at disadvantage.

### **Spellcasting**
12th-level spellcaster.  
**At Will:** *chill touch, mage hand, minor illusion, detect magic*  
**3/Day Each:** *blight, counterspell, fear, blight*  
**2/Day Each:** *blight; blinding smite* (necrotic); *summon undead*  
**1/Day Each:** *finger of death*

> **Blight Obsession.** *Blight* can deal necrotic damage; undead allies in the area heal HP equal to half the damage dealt.

### **Actions**
**Grave Bolt.** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +10 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (5d8) necrotic; target can’t regain HP until the start of the Bone Seer’s next turn.  
**Sovereign of Stillness (Recharge 5–6).** Creatures of choice within 30 ft. must succeed on a DC 18 Wis save or be restrained until the end of their next turn; on a success, speed is halved.

### **Legendary Actions** (3/round)
The Bone Seer can take one legendary action at a time, at the end of another creature’s turn.  
**Cantrip.** Cast *chill touch*.  
**Fade Step.** Teleport up to 20 ft. to dim light or darkness.  
**Grave Pulse (Costs 2).** Creatures of choice within 15 ft. take 10 (3d6) necrotic damage (DC 18 Con save for half).

### **Lair Actions (Withered Glade)**
On initiative count 20 (losing ties), one effect occurs:
- **Rooted Silence.** A 20-ft. radius becomes difficult terrain until the next round; enemies have disadvantage on concentration saves.
- **Necrotic Bloom.** A blighted tree erupts; enemies within 15 ft. take 14 (4d6) necrotic damage (DC 18 Con save for half).
- **Judgment of the Dead.** One restrained or frightened creature takes 18 (4d8) necrotic damage.
