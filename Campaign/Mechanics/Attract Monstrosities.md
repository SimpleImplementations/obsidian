**Attract Monstrosities**  
_5th-level enchantment (ritual)_  
**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Self (300-foot radius)  
**Components:** V, S, M (a severed limb or organ from a monstrosity worth at least 10 gp)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

You release a pungent magical lure that tugs at the instincts of nearby monstrosities. Roll 24d10; the total is how many hit points of creatures this spell can affect. Creatures are affected in ascending order of their current hit points (ignoring unconscious creatures), chosen from among monstrosities within 300 feet of you.

Each affected monstrosity must have an Intelligence of 9 or lower or it is immune to this effect.

Affected creatures are charmed and compelled to move toward you by the safest available route. When a creature reaches a space within 10 feet of you, it lingers there, drawn by the magical lure. The spell does not alter its disposition or behavior beyond this attraction. If an affected creature takes damage, the spell ends for that creature.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, roll an additional 8d10 for each slot level above 5th.

**Classes:** Druid, Ranger
