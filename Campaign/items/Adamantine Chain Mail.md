 
 

Armor (chain mail), uncommon

This suit of armor is reinforced with adamantine, one of the hardest substances in existence. While you’re wearing it, any [Critical Hit](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#CriticalHit) against you becomes a normal hit.

Made of interlocking metal rings, chain mail includes a layer of quilted fabric worn underneath the mail to prevent chafing and to cushion the impact of blows. The suit includes gauntlets.

Notes: Immunity: Critical Hits, Combat, Warding, Str. 13 Required, Stealth Disadvantage
