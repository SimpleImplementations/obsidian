**Verdant Living Armor**  
_Light Armor, very rare (attunement by druid)_  
**AC:** 13 + Dex

**Verdant Camouflage.** In forested or plant-rich environments, you have advantage on Stealth and leave no tracks.

**Radiant Immunity.** Immune to radiant damage. Being struck by a 3th-level or higher radiant spell causes all withered components to regrow.

**Leafguard.** When hit by a critical hit, the leaves flare into a burst of color and the attack becomes a normal hit. The leaves then wither and fall like autumn foliage until regrown.

**Moss Mend.** While below half HP, you regain 1d6 HP at the start of your turn for 1 minute. This ends early if you reach 0 HP. The moss dries and browns until regrown.

**Rootbind.** If reduced to 0 HP by nonmagical physical damage, roots cover you, leaving you unconscious but stable. The roots then wither until regrown. 1 de vida después del minuto

**Regrowth.** Components return after 8 hours in sunlight.
