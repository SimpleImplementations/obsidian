**Shield, Legendary (requires attunement by a good-aligned cleric or paladin)**

This shield gleams with the warm light of dawn, its polished surface etched with the holy symbol of Lathander. It radiates divine protection, standing as a beacon against the forces of darkness.

- **Radiant Aegis.** You gain a **+2 bonus to AC** while wielding this shield. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC.
    
- **Warding Suns.** When you or a creature within 10 feet of you is hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to invoke the shield’s radiant ward. The chosen creature gains a **+6 bonus to AC** and **resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage (including from magical weapons)** until the start of your next turn. Once you use this property, you can’t use it again until you finish a long rest.
    
- **Aura of the Light.** While holding this shield, you emit a **10-foot aura** of soft golden light. Allies within the aura have **advantage on saving throws against being frightened** and **resistance to necrotic damage**.
