*Weapon (mace), legendary (requires attunement by a cleric or paladin)*

### Weapon Properties
- +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon  
- Damage: 1d6 bludgeoning (versatile 1d8)

### Divine Enhancements

**Empowered Healing**  
When you cast a spell that restores hit points, add **twice your spellcasting ability modifier** to the healing instead of once.

**Radiant Focus**  
You gain a **+1 bonus to your spell save DC**.

**Aura of the Light**  
While holding this shield, you emit a **10-foot aura** of soft golden light.
