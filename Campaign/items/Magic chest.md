### **Adamantine Chest**

_Wondrous Item, Uncommon_

This 2 ft. by 3 ft. chest is forged entirely of adamantine and weighs 75 pounds when empty.

- **Indestructible Frame.** The chest is immune to damage from nonmagical weapons and cannot be broken open by mundane force.
    
- **Secure Lock.** The chest comes with a masterworked adamantine key lock. It can be unlocked normally with its key.
    
- **Lockpicking.** A creature proficient with thieves’ tools can attempt to pick the lock. CD 30 On a failure by 5 or more, the chest emits a faint humming sound audible within 20 feet. On a natural 1, the lockpicks jam, requiring 1 minute to reset.
    
- **Anti-Theft Ward.** If moved more than 30 feet from where it was last locked, the chest hums faintly until opened or relocked.
    
- **Capacity.** The chest holds only what can physically fit inside; it has no extradimensional properties.
